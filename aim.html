Aim of the Experiment

Virtual Laboratory on Jacobians

To develop an interactive virtual laboratory for understanding and computing Jacobians of functions of two or more variables.

Objectives

  • To understand the concept of partial differentiation.
  • To construct the Jacobian matrix for a given set of functions.
  • To evaluate the Jacobian determinant.
  • To determine whether functions are dependent or independent.
  • To verify manually calculated derivatives and determinants using the simulation.
  • To strengthen conceptual understanding through theory, pretests, and posttests.
  • To apply Jacobians in coordinate transformations and engineering problems.

Expected Learning Outcomes

  • Students will be able to compute first-order partial derivatives accurately.
  • Students will be able to form 2×2 and 3×3 Jacobian matrices.
  • Students will be able to evaluate determinants of Jacobians.
  • Students will be able to identify whether functions are dependent or independent.
  • Students will gain confidence in solving Jacobian problems from basic to advanced level.
